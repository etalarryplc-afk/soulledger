# Soul Ledger - Crypto/Forex Asset Recovery Website Specification

## Project Overview
- **Project Name**: Soul Ledger Website
- **Type**: Single-page marketing website with FAQ
- **Core Functionality**: Professional landing page for a crypto/forex asset recovery company with services info, trust indicators, and comprehensive FAQ
- **Target Users**: Individuals who have lost crypto/forex assets to scams, hacks, or failed platforms

## Brand Identity
- **Company Name**: Soul Ledger
- **Tagline**: "Recover What's Yours"
- **Brand Colors**:
  - Primary Black: `#0a0a0f` (deep black)
  - Secondary Black: `#151520` (dark charcoal)
  - Primary Blue: `#2563eb` (vibrant blue)
  - Accent Blue: `#3b82f6` (lighter blue)
  - White: `#ffffff`
  - Off-White: `#f8fafc`
  - Gray: `#94a3b8`

## UI/UX Specification

### Layout Structure
1. **Header**: Fixed navigation with logo, nav links, CTA button
2. **Hero Section**: Bold headline, subtext, dual CTAs, animated background
3. **Stats Bar**: Trust indicators with animated counters
4. **Services Section**: Grid of 4 service cards with icons
5. **Process Section**: 4-step recovery process with timeline
6. **Why Choose Us**: 3-column feature comparison
7. **Testimonials**: Carousel of client success stories
8. **FAQ Section**: Expandable accordion with 12+ questions
9. **Contact CTA**: Final call-to-action with form
10. **Footer**: Links, social icons, legal disclaimers

### Visual Design
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: "Outfit" (bold, modern geometric sans)
  - Body: "DM Sans" (clean, readable)
- **Spacing**: 8px base unit, generous whitespace
- **Visual Effects**:
  - Subtle gradient overlays
  - Glowing blue accents on hover
  - Smooth scroll behavior
  - Card hover lift effects
  - Animated number counters

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px (single column)
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px (2 columns)
- Desktop: > 1024px (full layout)
